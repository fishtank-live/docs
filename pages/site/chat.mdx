Viewer's of Fishtank.live have 24/7 access to our live Chat feature which allows you to converse with fellow Fishtank Fanatics, plot war with your [Clan](https://fishtanklive.mintlify.app/pages/features/clans), as well as complete various [Missions ](https://fishtanklive.mintlify.app/pages/features/missions)assigned to you.

To start chatting, simply type out your message in the chat box and smack that enter key!

![](/CHAT.png)

You can click on a user's name in the chat in order to interact with said user:

![](/interact.png)

To send emojis in chat, or show of Medals you have accumulated, select on either of the highlighted buttons below:

![](/emojimedals.png)

Simply select the Medal or Emoji you would like to share in the chat and press enter!
