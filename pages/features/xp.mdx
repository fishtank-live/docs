## Daily Streaks

Viewers can gain bonus XP on a daily basis by ensuring they click the 'Daily Bonus XP' button on the left-hand menu.&#x20;

![](/DAILYBONUSXP.png)

Consecutive days of logging in will gain **additional** bonus XP so make sure to get that streak going!

![](/xp.png)

## While you were gone

Random XP events can happen to you while you're away from the Fishtank. These will be displayed to you upon logging back in.

![](/WHILEYOUWEREGONE.png)
